What is ‚ÄúNeuroscience,‚Äù really?

Neuroscience is the study of the nervous system ‚Äî how the brain is built (structure) and how it works (function).

In this program, you‚Äôll use:

  • Structural MRI ‚Üí high-resolution pictures of anatomy (the map).
  • Functional MRI (fMRI) ‚Üí color overlays showing where activity increases during tasks (the traffic).
  • tDCS ‚Üí gentle, non-invasive currents via scalp electrodes to modulate networks.
  • TMS ‚Üí magnetic pulses that briefly activate cortex in a precise spot (‚Äúthe click‚Äù).
Myth-buster: fMRI is not mind reading; it measures oxygen-level changes (BOLD) related to neural activity.

Why these tools together?
  • Maps + effects: MRI/fMRI tell you where/when; tDCS/TMS test what happens if we nudge here.
  • Non-invasive & safe: we follow screening, intensity, and duration guidelines. No surgery, no shocks.
